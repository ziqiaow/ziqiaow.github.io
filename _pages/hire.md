#####  __Graduate students__

If you're interested in joining our graduate program, we invite you to apply here: [UVA Biomedical Sciences PhD program](https://med.virginia.edu/bims/). Graduate students are welcome to do rotations in our lab.

#####  __Postdoctoral research associates__
We have openings for postdoctoral research associates. To apply, please send your CV to ziqiao.wang@virginia.edu.


Research directions include but not limited to multi-omics data integration, polygenic risk score analysis, modeling of gene–environment interactions, and Mendelian randomization studies for causal inference. There will be opportunities to collaborate with the Department of Statistics, School of Data Science, and UVA Comprehensive Cancer Center.
