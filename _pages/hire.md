#####  __Graduate students__

If you're interested in joining our graduate program and joining my lab, please get in touch with me directly. For more information about our PhD program and to apply, please visit [UVA Biomedical Sciences PhD program](https://med.virginia.edu/bims/). I am currently accepting graduate students and rotation students.

#####  __Postdoctoral research associates__
We have openings for postdoctoral research associates. If you are interested in the research going on in the lab and want to discuss the possibility of working together, please be in touch. To apply, please send your CV to ziqiao.wang@virginia.edu.


Research directions include but not limited to multi-omics data integration, polygenic risk score analysis, modeling of gene–environment interactions, and Mendelian randomization studies for causal inference. There will be opportunities to collaborate with the Department of Statistics, School of Data Science, and UVA Comprehensive Cancer Center.
